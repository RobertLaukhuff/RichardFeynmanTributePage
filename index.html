Who is Richard Feynman?

  • Born on May 11, 1918 in New York, New York
  • Died of cancer on February 15, 1988 in Las Angeles, California
  • He studied physics at MIT and later received a doctorate from Princeton University
  • He worked on the Manhattan Project during World War II
  • He corrected errors in earlier formulas of quantum electrodynamics
  • He was part of the commission that investigated the space shuttle Challenger explosion
